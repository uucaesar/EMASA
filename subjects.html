"Don’t let Maths stand between you and your dream degree."

Mathematics is the ultimate gateway subject. At EMASA, we take the fear out of Maths by breaking down abstract concepts into logical, step-by-step methods. We cover the full syllabus, equipping learners to handle both the technical calculations of Paper 1 and the spatial reasoning for Paper 2.

  • Junior Phase (Grades 8 & 9): Building an unshakable foundation in Algebra and Geometry.
  • Senior Phase (Grades 10–12): Functions, Trigonometry, and Differential Calculus.

Sciences

Physics & Chemistry
"Understanding the mechanics of the world, one reaction at a time."

Physical Science is a content-rich subject. At EMASA, our goal is true comprehension over rote memorisation. Once a learner understands *why* a principle works, applying the formula becomes second nature. We split our focus equally between Physics and Chemistry.

  • Physics (Paper 1): Newton’s Laws, Work, Energy, and Electrodynamics.
  • Chemistry (Paper 2): Stoichiometry, Equilibrium, and Organic Chemistry.
  • Practical Application: Linking theory to exam-tested experiments.
